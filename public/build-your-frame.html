Frequently Asked Questions
Here are some frequently asked questions about the Frame Builder
Can I get my photos printed in colour or black and white?
Yes, you can add instructions like that in the comment box.
Can I have a wider or narrower mount around my image?
Yes, you can add instructions like that in the comment box.
Do you have a different profile/colour frame available?
We have selected the most contemporary frames for the FrameBuilder, however, if you prefer a different frame, you can write a description of it in the comment box and we will get back to you with availability.
I already have a printed photo/s, can I still use the FrameBuilder?
This great tool is designed for professionally print and frame your beautiful memories. If you already have your prints then just send us an email to hello@framexperts.com to get your quotation for framing.
What is the earliest I can get my order delivered?
We advise you to allow a minimum of two weeks for your order to be shipped. You can opt to collect from our studio in Ranelagh in just 2-3 days!
How long does shipping take?
Usually about a week. In some countries it takes a little longer.
Is my frame(s) ready to be installed?
Yes, we put appropriate cord, wire or hanging fittings at the back.
Do you ship internationally?
We ship to most countries around the world.
Quality Assurance
Here is a brief description of the great materials we use to make your frame.
Quality Solid Wood Frames
Choose from our specially selected range of quality solid wood mouldings to create your perfect frame. Contemporary colours with a subtle wood grain visible give these mouldings a unique natural feel. Measuring 22mm on the face and 22mm deep.
Conservation White Core Mountboard
Our mountboards are 100% Conservation quality that will not discolour with age and is bleed resistant, pure White bevel that stays white.
Our Printer, Ink & Paper
UltraChrome HDX pigment inks: Delivers highly-accurate colours with 98% of Pantone®, Ensuring consistent results and high-quality, reliable output with superior lightfastness: Prints on our Premium Photosatin Paper can last up to 60 years.
Anti Reflective Glass
Our anti reflective glass reveals the true colours of the image, no green tint or optical distortions. Thanks to its anti-reflective coating, which reduces unwanted reflections to less than 1%, it has a smooth surface that allows for details to emerge unaltered. its coating is scratch resistant and very easy to clean.
